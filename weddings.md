# Wedding Ceremonies Business

## Overview
- **Owner:** Camilo's wife
- **Service:** Wedding officiant — performs ceremonies, obtains marriage license from court, files paperwork
- **Locations:** Homes, parks, beaches, anywhere in the area
- **Languages:** English & Spanish (bilingual ceremonies)
- **Market:** Oceanside / North County San Diego / San Diego County (~22,700 weddings/year)
- **Status:** Pre-launch — no social media presence yet

## Differentiators
- Bilingual (English + Spanish) — underserved niche in SD
- All-in-one: ceremony + license + court filing
- Mobile — travels to client's location
- Beach ceremonies in Oceanside (high demand)
